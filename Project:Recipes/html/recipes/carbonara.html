Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara

An absolute classic, carbonara only uses bacon, eggs, parmesan cheese and cooking water to get it just right.

Follow the step by step carefully to ensure a very creamy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chopped bacon to taste
  • 3 Eggs
  • Black papper to taste
  • 1 Milk cream
  • Grated cheese to taste
  • Salt
  • Spaghetti pasta

Preparing:

  1. Fry the bacon until crispy
  2. Put the pasta to cook in salt and water
  3. In the dish where the pasta will be served, beat the eggs well with a fork.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and add the grated cheese, also to taste.
  5. When the pasta is ready, drain it and add (very hot) the remaining egg mixture and mix well.
  6. The heat from the dough will cook the eggs.
  7. Place the bacon over the pasta and serve.
