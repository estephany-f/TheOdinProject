Strawberry Milk Shake

Takes just a few minutes and tastes delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 2 Scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Â½ Cup milk (any type)
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)

Preparing:

  1. Blend the ingredients in a blender, combine the strawberries, vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract (if using).
  2. Blend until smooth. Blend until the mixture is creamy and smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream if desired.

Enjoy your refreshing strawberry milkshake!
